Iron Man

Bio

Full Name: Tony Stark

Alias: Iron Man

Place of Birth: Long Island, New York

Race: Human

Occupation: Inventor, Industrialist, Superhero

Abilities: Genius-level intellect, expert engineer, skilled hand-to-hand combatant

Weapons: Powered armor suit, repulsor rays, missiles, lasers

Affiliation: Avengers, Stark Industries


Power Stats

Combat Skills: 85

Intelligence: 100

Strength: 85

Speed: 75

Durability: 85


Trivia

  • Iron Man first appeared in Tales of Suspense #39 in 1963.
  • The character was created by Stan Lee, Larry Lieber, Don Heck, and Jack Kirby.
  • Tony Stark's suit has over 50 different versions in the comics and movies.
  • Robert Downey Jr. portrayed Iron Man in the Marvel Cinematic Universe.
  • Iron Man is a founding member of the Avengers.
