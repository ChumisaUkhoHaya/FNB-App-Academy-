Black Widow

Bio

Full Name: Natalia Alianovna Romanova (Natasha Romanoff)

Alias: Black Widow

Place of Birth: Stalingrad, Russia

Race: Human (Enhanced)

Occupation: Spy, Assassin, Avenger

Abilities: Master martial artist, expert tactician, skilled acrobat, espionage, slowed aging, enhanced immune system

Weapons: Widow's Bite, batons, firearms, gadgets

Affiliation: Avengers, S.H.I.E.L.D., Red Room

First Appearance: Tales of Suspense #52 (1964)

Creators: Stan Lee, Don Rico, Don Heck

Notable Enemies: Taskmaster, Red Room, HYDRA, General Dreykov

Notable Allies: Hawkeye, Captain America, Iron Man, Nick Fury

Trivia

  • Black Widow was originally a Russian spy before defecting to S.H.I.E.L.D.
