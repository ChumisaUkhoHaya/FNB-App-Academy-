Hawkeye

Bio

Full Name: Clinton Francis Barton

Alias: Hawkeye

Place of Birth: Waverly, Iowa

Race: Human

Occupation: Master Archer, S.H.I.E.L.D. Agent

Abilities: Master archer, expert marksman, skilled hand-to-hand combatant, acrobat

Weapons: Bow and trick arrows

Affiliation: Avengers, S.H.I.E.L.D.


Power Stats

Power Level: 55

Combat Skills: 95

Intelligence: 75

Strength: 35

Speed: 40

Durability: 35

Energy Projection: 10


Trivia

  • Hawkeye first appeared in Marvel Comics in 1964.
  • He was originally introduced as a villain before joining the Avengers.
  • Hawkeye is known for never missing a shot with his bow and arrows.
  • Despite having no superhuman powers, he is one of the most skilled members of the Avengers.
