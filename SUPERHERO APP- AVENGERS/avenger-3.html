Alias: Hulk

Race: Human (Gamma Mutate)

Abilities: Incredible superhuman strength, durability, regeneration, and anger empowerment

Trivia

  • Hulk first appeared in The Incredible Hulk #1 in 1962.
  • His iconic green color was originally gray in his first comic appearance.
  • The madder Hulk gets, the stronger he becomes.
  • Bruce Banner and Hulk have been separated into two different beings in some storylines.
  • Hulk has been a member of several superhero teams, including the Defenders and the Avengers.
